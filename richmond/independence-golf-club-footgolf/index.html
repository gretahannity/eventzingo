Richmond FootGolf

Soccer Meets Golf at Independence Golf Club

What is FootGolf?

Combining the skill of soccer with the precision of golf, FootGolf is played on a golf course with a size 5 soccer ball. The object? Kick the ball into 21-inch diameter cups in as few shots as possible!

The Independence Course

Independence Golf Club features one of the most premier FootGolf layouts in the Mid-Atlantic. Located in Midlothian, just south of Richmond, it’s the perfect venue for team building, birthday parties, or a fun afternoon with friends.

Key Highlights

  • All Skill Levels: No golf experience required—if you can kick a ball, you can play!
  • Great for Groups: Perfect for soccer teams and social clubs.
  • Beautiful Scenery: Play on the rolling hills of the Independence Short Course.
